Suguna RIPS welcomes you…..

Most great things have humble beginnings and our school is no exception. Suguna RIP V School was established in 1987 by our founder, Shri K. Venkatesalu, an industrialist and philanthropist. His commitment to the welfare of the society acted as an impetus for the establishment of this school. The founders, Shri K. Venkatesalu and Smt. Rukmani Ammal, in their vision, foresaw an educational institution which would provide a rich learning experience to help students develop their talents and potentials, to make them world citizens. The silver jubilee of our school was celebrated on 24th August 2012 and the pearl jubilee on 24th August 2017. We march forward with bigger dreams rising above expectations and seizing opportunities to reach the next milestone. Treading through the path of excellence and supplemented by a select group of efficient, effective, well-qualified and enterprising staff, the school has grown into a full-fledged edifice of learning.
